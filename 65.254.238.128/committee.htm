 Committees

 

Relations and Co-Operation Committee

Chairman: - Saleh Said Massan

Tasks:-
  • Strengthening the relations and enhancing the scientific and technical cooperation between economists in the Sultanate and them and their colleagues in the Arab countries and the rest of the world.
  •  Exchanging visits and information with the other professional associations and institutions.
  •  Supervising the organisational aspects of conducting conferences lectures and training programmes
  •  Media Relations

Professional Development Committee

Chairman: - Dr. Said Mohamed Ahmad Alsaqri
Tasks:-
  • Contributing in organizing the basis of the economic consultancy profession and raising its standard in cooperation with the other concerned authorities.
  • Urging members to maintain the ethics of the profession and raising its standards.
  •  Representing the professional interests of its members.
  •  Tracking memberships

Studies and Research Committee

Chairman: - Dr. Mohammed Rashid Saif Al Jahwari

Tasks:-
  •  Activating, encouraging and publishing scientific research in order to contribute the socio-economic development activities in Oman.
  • Cooperating with the government and private agencies concerned with economic development provision of experts and stating opinions in economic matters if asked.
  •  Supervising the academic aspects of conducting conferences , lectures and training programmes
  •  Supervising the Oman Business Confidence Index affairs.
  •  Supervising the Association Award affairs.
