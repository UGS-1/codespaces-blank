Conference on the New Economy
Tuesday 27 September 2005- Wednesday 28 September 2005

 



Conference objectives
ï To give prominence to the crucial role of the private sector in economic development.
ï To make awareness of the concept of the new economy which is based on knowledge and to identify the new opportunities.
ï To diagnose challenges which impede the development of private sector activity.
ï To benefit from experiences of other countries and international organizations in developing the private sector

Provisional Conference Programme
The first session: The Economic frame for an efficient performance of the public and private sectors.
The second session: The role of a knowledge based economy in reinforcing competition and raising efficiency.
The third session: Personal initiatives and their role in developing the private sector.
The fourth session: The role of small and medium enterprises in developing economic activity.
The fifth session: The role of financing institutions in developing the private sector.
The sixth session: Globalization: opportunities and challenges before the private sector.
The seventh session: Privatization and its role in developing the private sector.
The eighth session: The contribution of the legislative and organizational environment in the development of private sector activity.

Participants:
ï Chief administrative staff at ministries and government institutions.
ï Chief administrative staff from the private sector.
ï Teaching staff at universities, institutes and research centres.
ï Experts in regional and international organizations.

Condition of the researches to be submitted:
ï The deadline for submitting researches is Tuesday 21 May 2005.
ï Researches will be accepted in both Arabic and English.
ï All researches shall be subjected to academic judgment.


An Abstract of not more than 100 words in both Arabic and English of the Research should be attached with it.
