The Association Award
for Economic Research
 

The Association Board decided to offer annual awards for economic researches, which are to be delivered during the convening of the Associationís annual conference.

The award objectives:
 

- Reinforcing and encouraging economic research to support the process of development in Oman.
- Urging Omani and expatriate researchers to conduct academic researches of interest to the country.
- Establishing a research base in order to assist decision makers and to reinforce the role of the Association as an effective partner in the comprehensive development of the country.

The Research Area

The focus should be on new applied economic researches and to present proposal for future development in Oman.

Research topics
Human resource development, education and training, small entrepreneurs, finance and banking, commerce and industry, transportation and shipping, tourism, communication and information technology, health services, combat of poverty, social cooperation, assessing various government programmes, investment environment in Oman, systems and mechanism of various government Sectors functioning, methods and means of income diversification.

Eligible categories: The award is opened to Omanis and researchers from other nationalities.
Awards: Three proposed awards are to be given from each of the following categories:

General category: 1000 ñ 2000 ñ 3000 OR
Student category: 200 ñ 300 ñ500 OR
